# Brand Growth Scorecard

## Primary objectives

- Grow qualified traffic to `noahlucas.com`
- Strengthen brand authority as VP Product + entrepreneur

## Monthly KPIs

- Total sessions
- Unique visitors
- Returning visitor rate
- Top 5 landing pages
- Top 5 referrers
- Notes post performance (views + engagement)
- Profile/CTA clicks (LinkedIn, contact, newsletter if added)

## Quality indicators

- Long-session share (engaged sessions)
- Scroll depth on key pages
- Notes publish consistency
- Conversion proxy trend (inquiry clicks, outbound profile clicks)

## Review format

- What grew
- What stalled
- Hypothesis for why
- Next 2 experiments
