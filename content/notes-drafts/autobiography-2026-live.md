## March 2026 · Live

# Beginning the Living Record

Ojai is home. I’m living with Sara and Willa; Bruce the cat is here too. The center of gravity is family, not career theater. That is the point of this season.

Work is heavy. I’m serving as VP of Product at Sift, with the mix of product judgment and organizational execution that can fill every hour if I let it. The daily question is how to do serious work without letting ambition flatten the life at home.

Mid-month I started building a living autobiographer. Not a brand exercise, but a hedge against ambient forgetting—the way good, ordinary days vanish when no one writes them down. The aim is simple: remember faithfully so the people closest to me can later know what this felt like.

I’m trying to hold work and family in the same frame, not as parallel stories but as one life. The writing is part of that—naming what matters while it’s still fresh.

- Can I keep a steady rhythm of notes without turning family into content?
- Which habits at Sift help me lead well and still come home present?
- What small, ordinary moments are worth capturing next month?
